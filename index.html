About Liam Jakubowski

I am driven by a passion for problem-solving and connection. I believe that software engineering is one of the fastest and most accessible ways to distribute meaningful solutions to large groups of people. I want to build intuitive, secure, and long-lasting tools that guide and empower others.

At the same time, I am a lifelong musician. I believe music connects people on a level that words alone cannot reach. Music teaches resilience, leadership, collaboration, and the ability to perform under pressure — skills that deeply inform my approach to both technology and life.

In the future, I want to explore ethical AI integration, particularly within music and life-enriching experiences. My goal is to help bridge the gap between human creativity and intelligent systems in a way that uplifts communities and builds stronger connections.

Why Software & Music

I am passionate about problem-solving through software because it’s a powerful way to create scalable, affordable, and widely accessible solutions. Software connects industries, people, and ideas — it holds our modern world together.

I am equally passionate about music’s ability to connect people without words. In music, you must strive for excellence every time — there is no “80% performance.” That drive has shaped my reliability, my leadership, and my ability to perform under pressure.

Through both software and music, I aim to build systems that connect, empower, and inspire people.
